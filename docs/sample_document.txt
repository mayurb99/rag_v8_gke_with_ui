Company Onboarding Guide

Welcome to TechCorp! This guide will help you get started.

First Day:
- Report to HR at 9 AM
- Get your access card
- Meet your team lead

First Week:
- Complete security training
- Set up your development environment
- Join all relevant Slack channels

Employee Benefits:
- Health insurance (company pays 80%)
- 401k match (up to 5%)
- Remote work policy (2 days per week)
- Annual learning budget ($2000)

Work Hours and Policies:
- Standard hours: 9 AM to 6 PM
- Flexible schedule available
- Work from home up to 3 days per week
- Lunch break: 1 hour (paid)
- No mandatory overtime
